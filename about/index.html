About

Headquartered in Texas and serving domestic and international destinations from our Fort Wayne, Indiana distribution center, IASA maintains the following quality control and business certifications, representations and accreditations.

IASA GROUP, LLC is a world-wide, value-added supplier of aircraft parts, components, accessories aviation services and consumable products to the military, governmental agencies, airlines, private aircraft operators, corporate fleets, airframe manufacturers and maintenance repair and overhaul operations.

Through a world-wide network of offices and agents, IASA supplies aircraft parts and aviation services to: US Military, Foreign Military, Government Agencies, OEM’s, Commercial Operators, Maintenance Repair and Overhaul Organizations, & Corporate Aircraft Operators.

IASA, established in 1999 in Dallas, Texas is a registered Minority/Women Owned Small Business. Today following more than a decade of growth, IASA is proud to have grown into North America’s key distributor of Trellelborg Sealing Solutions. At IASA Group, we have redefined consumable kitting solutions allowing our customers to maximize dispatch reliability and mission readiness while we focus on minimizing parts handling and maximizing inventory control for the absolute lowest cost of custom tailored kitting anywhere.
